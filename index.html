    About Our Taste

    Mittal's Kitchen was Established by Shri Kumar Mittal in the year 1850, this shop is located in Paltan Bazar, DehraDun. The shop could be called as the brand ambassador of ‘Haldiram...’, a snack served with Chai and a household favourite with three time meal all over DehraDun. It is a savoury prepared with crispy gram flour Chole-Bhature,Rajma-Chawal,Madrasi-Dosa,Naan-Paneer....spices and pure Desi ghee. The recipe for Mittalji’s Naan,Bhature has been preserved for over six generations and this makes it exclusive and eternal!...with Special Kesar Kulfi....❤️

    Chole Bhature

    It is a combination of chana masala (spicy white chickpeas) and bhatura/puri, a fried bread made from maida.Although it is known as a typical Punjabi dish,there are varied claims around the origin of dish.

    Naan-Paneer

    A delightful Indian Naan that is stuffed with a classic and delectable mixture of Indian paneer, onions and some spices. It can be served as breakfast and also as a main meal.
